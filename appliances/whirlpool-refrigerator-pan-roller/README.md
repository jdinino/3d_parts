## Symptoms of Failure

- Drawer sticks or drags
- Drawer tilts to one side
- Grinding noise when opening/closing
- Drawer jumps off track

## Compatible Refrigerator Models

### Whirlpool
- Side-by-side and French door refrigerators with pan drawers

### KitchenAid
- Side-by-side and French door refrigerators with pan drawers

### Maytag
- Side-by-side and French door refrigerators with pan drawers

### Kenmore / Kenmore Elite
- Whirlpool-manufactured refrigerators with pan drawers
